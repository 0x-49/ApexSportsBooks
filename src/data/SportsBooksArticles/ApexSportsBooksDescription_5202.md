## II. A Brief History of Betfred

### Foundation and Growth

Betfred was founded in 1967 by Fred Done and his brother Peter in Salford, England. Initially operating as a single retail betting shop, the company gradually expanded throughout the UK, leveraging excellent customer service and a genuine passion for sports betting. The brand became known for its competitive odds and innovative promotional offers, rapidly gaining popularity among betting enthusiasts.

**Key Milestones:**

- **2004:** Betfred launched its online sportsbook, extending its reach to a wider audience and embracing the digital age of gambling.
- **2010:** The company acquired the Tote, a government-licensed pool betting operator in the UK, further solidifying its market position.
- **2012:** Betfred opened more betting shops, expanding its physical presence and maintaining one of the largest retail networks in the UK.
- **2018:** The company was awarded a license from the New Jersey Division of Gaming Enforcement, marking its entry into the regulated US market.

### Current Standing

Today, Betfred operates both online and via a vast network of retail outlets, boasting millions of customers across various platforms. The brand has a loyal following, thanks to its strong reputation, commitment to responsible gambling, and dedication to customer satisfaction. Betfred is often prominently featured during major sports events, offering enticing promotional offers and enhanced odds.

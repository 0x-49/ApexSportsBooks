## II. A Brief History of FanDuel

### Foundation and Growth

FanDuel was founded in 2009 by Nigel Eccles, Lesley Eccles, and a group of fellow tech enthusiasts. Originally conceived as a daily fantasy sports (DFS) platform, FanDuel quickly gained popularity due to its user-friendly interface and engaging gameplay. Over the years, it expanded its offerings and became a leader in the DFS market.

**Key Milestones:**

- **2012:** Established itself as a major player in the DFS landscape, raising significant venture capital funding.
- **2018:** Launched its sports betting platform following the repeal of PASPA (Professional and Amateur Sports Protection Act), enabling states to legalize sports gambling.
- **2020:** Merged with gaming operator Flutter Entertainment, further solidifying its position in the market.

**Current Standing:**

Today, FanDuel is recognized as one of the most trusted and innovative sports betting platforms in the United States, offering a wide range of betting options, promotions, and a robust mobile app.

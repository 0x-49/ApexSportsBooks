## II. A Brief History of Ladbrokes

### Foundation and Evolution

Ladbrokes has a storied history that dates back to its founding in 1886 by a group of horse racing enthusiasts. Over the decades, it has grown from a small operation, primarily focused on horse racing, into a formidable force in global betting, expanding its offerings to include sports like football, rugby, tennis, and more.

**Key Milestones:**

- **1961:** Ladbrokes made history by being one of the first companies to receive a betting license under the Betting and Gaming Act.
- **2000:** The introduction of online betting opened up a new avenue for growth, with a website that allowed bettors to place wagers from the comfort of their homes, thus making it a pioneer in the digital age.
- **2016:** Ladbrokes merged with Coral, which further solidified its market dominance, resulting in a combined entity known as Ladbrokes Coral Group, valued at approximately £2.3 billion.

**Modern Era:**

Today, Ladbrokes offers a seamless betting experience that includes a robust sportsbook, a variety of online casino games, and live betting features. Its user-centric approach aims to provide clients with a safe, transparent platform while embracing modern technology to enhance user experience.

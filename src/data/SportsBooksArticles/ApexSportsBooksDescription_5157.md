## II. A Brief History of Sports Coral

### Foundation and Growth

Sports Coral, a brand under the Coral Group, was established to meet the increased demand for online sports betting. Its roots trace back to 1926 when the Coral brand began as a bookmaker in the UK. Over the years, the brand transitioned into the online realm, capitalizing on the internet boom to reach a global audience.

**Milestones in the Brand’s Journey:**

- **1999:** Coral launched its first online platform, allowing customers to place bets from the comfort of their homes.
- **2005:** Acquired by Gala Coral Group, expanding its market presence across the UK.
- **2016:** The brand rebranded its online presence, focusing on user experience and innovation technology.
- **2018:** Coral merged with Ladbrokes, creating one of the largest betting entities in the UK.

**Current Standing:**

Today, Sports Coral is recognized as a reliable and competitive player in the online sports betting market. With an extensive array of offerings ranging from traditional sports betting to live betting and virtual sports, it continues to adapt and innovate to meet customer needs.

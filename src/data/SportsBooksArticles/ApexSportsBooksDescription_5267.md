**Standard Offer:**

- **Bonus Type:** Up to $500 in welcome bonus for new clients.
- **Eligibility:** Available on first deposits only.

### Understanding the Fine Print

**Wagering Requirements:**

- **Amount:** Typically a 20x turnover requirement for bonus funds.
- **Example:** If you deposit $200 and receive a $200 bonus, you'd need to trade a total of $4,000 to withdraw any bonus-derived profits.

**Time Restrictions:**

- **Validity Period:** Bonuses usually must be met within a specific timeframe, such as 30 days.
- **Inactivity Clause:** If no trading activity occurs within that period, the bonus may be forfeited.

### Terms Impact on Traders

**Pros:**

- **Extended Playtime:** The bonus can provide an excellent opportunity to explore the trading platform without risking large amounts of capital.
- **Incentive for New Traders:** Encourages new traders to begin their trading journey with added support.

**Cons:**

- **High Turnover Requirements:** The requirement of a 20x turnover can be challenging, especially for new traders.
- **Applicability to Different Instruments:** Not all trading instruments may contribute equally towards clearing the wagering requirements.
